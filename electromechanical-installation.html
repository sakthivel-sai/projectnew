Electromechanical Equipment Installation and Maintenance

We provide expert installation and maintenance of electromechanical equipment critical to building operations. This includes pumps, motors, fans, generators, control panels, and other MEP systems. Our technicians ensure precise alignment, wiring, calibration, and safety testing for all units. We also install automation systems and integrate them into BMS (Building Management Systems) for efficient energy use and monitoring. Regular maintenance contracts include inspections, troubleshooting, and part replacements, ensuring reliable performance and avoiding costly breakdowns. Our services are essential for commercial buildings, residential complexes, and industrial facilities where uptime, energy efficiency, and safety are paramount.

Key Features

  • Control panel installation and wiring
  • Generator and UPS integration
  • Scheduled servicing and fault diagnostics
  • Pumps, motors, fans, and compressors
